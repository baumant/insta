    We will create an experience that drops the user into the world of this mock social media platform — InstaVeil — and into a *fake* feed of videos that, as the user scrolls through them, are all *magically* synced to Veil Hymn’s debut song.

  • yungjake

    Most of said videos feature Dev and an original choreographed dance piece that unfolds over the course of the song’s runtime (which right now is about 4:20). Some videos are BTS snippets from Dev’s producing grind. Some are purely random or abstract and do not feature Dev at all. But regardless of the content of each video, the thread is woven together by the song and Dev’s dance, personalizing the feel of the experience in a unique way for every user.
